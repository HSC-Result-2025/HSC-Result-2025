The HSC Result 2025 has been released at 11.00 am. The Higher Secondary Certificate exam is one of the most important public exams in Bangladesh. Students and parents are very excited about the publication of results.

How to Check HSC Result 2025?

There are two main ways to check Bangladesh Education Board HSC result: Online & SMS. You can also check via mobile apps or your educational institution notice board.

Online Method

  1. Visit https://noticepointbd.my-board.org/hsc-result/
  2. Select HSC/Alim/Vocational Examination tab
  3. Select your Exam Year (2025) and Education Board
  4. Enter Roll number and Registration Number
  5. Type the security key and click "Submit"

SMS Method

  1. Compose a message: HSC [space] Board 3-letter code [space] Roll Number [space] Year
  2. Example: HSC DHA 934567 2025
  3. Send to 16222, receive SMS with your HSC result

FAQs — Frequently Asked Questions

HSC Result 2025 officially released on 15 October 2025 at 11.00 am.

কিভাবে অনলাইনে HSC রেজাল্ট দেখব?

Go to https://noticepointbd.my-board.org/hsc-result/, select board & exam, enter roll & registration number, click submit to view marks.
